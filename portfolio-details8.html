Visual Media Evaluation

   1. What is the message of this ad?
   The message of this advertisement conveys that despite bad rainy days, the audience's bag and other stuff will still be dry if they buy their backpack.

   2. Who is the target audience?
   The target audiences are backpackers and travelers.

   3. Is the graphic appropriate to its message and audience?
   No, the tone and style of the graphic are too dark and dull for their message.

   4. Are the elements in the ad (image, text, color, etc) in harmony?
   No. First, the colors are too dull. Second, the font sizes and emphasis are imbalanced, there are too many elements trying to get the audience's attention. Lastly, the rainy background is barely there to be noticed, it doesn't add anything to make the advertisement look captivating. In summary, there is no harmony between the elements because they are too crowded and there isn't one focus, one thing that the audience should notice first.

   5. If you could change an element in the ad, what would it be? (you can also say none if you think the ad is appropriate and has all the elements)
   I would change the colors used into lighter ones to give more life to the advertisement. The font sizes would also be changed to give proper emphasis to each line/phrase or element. Since it's an advertisement, the slogan should be emphasized more than the brand and product name.

   To keep up with the current design trend, it would be ideal to use a cart icon instead of the word "Shop". That is to lessen the use of words and to give a cleaner look. The last change that I would make is the replacement of the raining background image with a more noticeable picture or clipart. Hopefully, these changes would give a more balanced contrast to the picture.
